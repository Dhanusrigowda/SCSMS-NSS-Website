Community Work

NSS volunteers undertook a five-day community work program in ABC Village from 1st October to 5th October 2025. The camp was organized with the aim of serving the rural community. Volunteers actively participated in village cleanliness and sanitation activities. Awareness programs on health, hygiene, and education were conducted. Tree plantation and environmental protection activities were carried out. Students interacted with villagers to understand their problems and needs. The program helped in improving the village environment and social awareness. It also developed leadership, teamwork, and a sense of social responsibility among students.
